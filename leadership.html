Teaching Assistant — Mobile and Articulated Robots

ESPOL  ·  2S 2024 – 1S 2025

  • Mobile Robotics Labs: Designed practice sessions using Lego Mindstorms, Robotino, and custom ROS packages — covering kinematics, control logic, and sensor integration.
  • Articulated Arms: Supervised labs on ROS integration with ABB industrial arms — forward/inverse kinematics, motion planning, and trajectory execution.
  • Student Evaluation: Grading, technical feedback, and office hours support for undergraduate students.
