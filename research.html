Autonomous Navigation & Racing

High-Speed Navigation & Obstacle Avoidance | Roboracer Ecuador Champion

Race Highlights

Leading the AIROS team, we developed a high-speed navigation stack capable of dynamic obstacle avoidance. Additionally, in collaboration with SKKU (South Korea), I benchmarked perception pipelines using LiDAR and Depth Cameras.

  • Tech: Nav2, Slam_toolbox, Adaptive Monte Carlo Localization (AMCL).

  • Achievement: 1st Place at Roboracer Ecuador 2024.
